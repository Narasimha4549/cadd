Training & Certification Course

Introduction

Master Certificate in Product Design course is designed to equip students with essential skills for a successful career in product design. Covering engineering drawing, material selection, design concepts, and GD&T, the course emphasises aesthetics, functionality, and manufacturability. It includes Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) fundamentals and design validation techniques, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the product lifecycle from inception to disposal. Ideal for aspiring product designers, this course offers training in four different software combinations, allowing students to choose based on their preferences.

Scope of Course

The Master Certificate program trains students in a comprehensive range of knowledge sets and skills crucial in the mechanical engineering industry. The focus of the training includes:

  • Basics of Engineering Drawing
  • Material selection
  • Design concepts
  • Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T)
  • Engineering Drawing using AutoCAD
  • 3D Modelling: (Combinations available to choose from) SolidWorks + CATIA | SolidWorks + Creo | CATIA + NX | Creo + CATIA
  • Design validation concepts
  • Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) fundamentals

Students will be equipped to identify and resolve potential issues, optimize designs for efficient production, and gain expertise in essential CAD programs, making this Master Certificate course indispensable for mechanical engineers and designers.

Career Prospects / Job Roles

Engineering graduates can become Product Designers, Design Engineers, or Industrial Designers across various industries. The demand for engineers skilled in CAD tools like AutoCAD and SolidWorks is growing, valued for efficiency and innovation. With emerging mechanical industries, CAD expertise is increasingly sought after.

key Job roles include:

  • Product Designer
  • Design Engineer
  • Industrial Designer
  • CAD Designer
  • Packaging Designer
